Consultancy Fee

Interview Training Program

We all know the reality of B.Ed Colleges. We have a specialized Interview Training Program for teachers where we make them able to speak, able to answer, and most importantly, what not to speak.

Our Workflow

Once a teacher uploads their resume to our database, we will share relative opportunities with them. If they are interested, we will send the resume to the school owner.

  • Once the interview is scheduled, we will make you aware of the date, timings, and location.
  • We will take feedback from the school after your interview.
  • Once we get feedback, we will inform you to get ahead.

Our Message to Teachers
